* This is a Latin phrase that translates to "I do, therefore I am" or "I make, therefore I am"
* It means our identity is shaped by what we do or what we make, not just by having thoughts.
* In simple language **"We are what we do"**
* Internalizing this means you become a person of action. Without actions all that knowledge you consume will be of no use.  
  **Knowledge + Action = Wisdom**
* Some phrases which have similar connotation
	* Ideas are cheap , execution is expensive
	* Talk is cheap, Actions are everything.
	* "We are what we eat".  Our bodies are built by what we consume.
* In these world of information overload, we can't take action on everything. Thoughtful action is more effective.
	* Similar phrase -  "Pick your battles. Not battles are worth fighting."
